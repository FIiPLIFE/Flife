Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Flife ("the app") handles information.

1. Personal Information

Flife does not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Precise location
  • Payment information

2. Local Device Data

The app stores some settings locally on your device to provide functionality, including:

  • Total flip count
  • Pending global flip count
  • Sound setting
  • Total flip display setting

This information remains on your device unless the app is deleted.

3. Firebase Firestore

Flife uses Firebase Firestore to maintain a global flip counter. Only aggregated flip count increments are sent to Firebase. This information is not linked to your identity.

4. Analytics and Tracking

Flife does not use advertising identifiers, third-party advertising, analytics tools, or cross-app tracking technologies.

5. Sharing Feature

The app allows users to share result images and text using the iOS share sheet. Shared content is handled by the apps and services selected by the user.

6. Third-Party Services

Firebase Firestore is provided by Google Firebase. Their privacy practices may apply.

7. Children's Privacy

Flife is not intended to collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be reflected on this page.
